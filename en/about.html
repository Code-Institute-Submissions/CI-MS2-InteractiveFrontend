About:


Majgull Axelsson has been actively writing fiction since the early 90s and was an active journalist long before. Raised by a waitress and a locomotive-driver, her interest in the tumultous post-war era of Sweden has been a driving force in her authorship. She is currently published by Norstedts, with some of her earlier work still published by Brombergs. International editions of Majgulls work can be found on amazon.
If you're looking for English language resources or comments on Majgulls work, please refer first to the Goodreads page linked from here. If you have direct inquiries, please use the contact form on this site.



About the site:


This site is maintained by the webmaster, who you may contact with inquiries on the contact page. If you wish to contact Majgull directly, you may use the same form. All mail adressed to her will be forwarded to her, but she is unable to respond to every inquiry.
